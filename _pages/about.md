I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the [University of Sherbrooke](https://www.usherbrooke.ca/mathematiques/), under the supervision of [Prof. Maxence Mayrand](https://maxencemayrand.github.io/). Before that, I was a PhD student at the [University of Sidi Mohamed Ben Abdellah](https://www.usmba.ac.ma/), supervised by [Prof. Lahcen Oukhtite](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=fr&user=D2ZlnJIAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate).

My research interests lie at the intersection of algebra and geometry: derivations theory, algebraic geometry, Lie groups, topological quantum field theories, shifted symplectic geometry, and Poisson geometry.
